Privacy Policy

Gamer tags generated in TagSmith are assembled entirely on your own device, inside your browser, from a hardcoded word list. Nothing is ever uploaded to, or stored on, any server operated by TagSmith.

TagSmith uses standard web analytics to understand aggregate traffic (page views, referrers) and may display advertising through Google AdSense, which can set its own cookies subject to Google's own privacy policy.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the details on the About page.

Every gamer tag is generated locally in your browser. Nothing you type or generate is ever sent to a server.
