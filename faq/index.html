Frequently Asked Questions

Is TagSmith really free?

Yes. Every tag is generated on your own device from a hardcoded word list, so there is no server cost per generation and no reason to charge or limit usage.

Is anything I generate uploaded anywhere?

No. Tags are assembled locally in your browser. Nothing ever leaves your device.

What vibes are available?

Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Edgy, and Cute, each pulls from its own curated list of adjectives and nouns so the tags actually match the mood you want.

Can I control the length and characters?

Yes. Toggle numbers and special characters on or off, add the classic xX…Xx wrapper, and drag the max-length slider so every tag fits under your platform username cap. Tags are re-rolled until they fit.

How do favorites work?

Click the star on any tag you like and it is saved to your device using localStorage. Your favorites stay put between visits and never touch a server. Clear them any time.

Will two people ever get the exact same tag?

It is possible but unlikely, each vibe combines dozens of adjectives with dozens of nouns, plus optional numbers and separators, for thousands of combinations. Click Generate again for a fresh one.

Every gamer tag is generated locally in your browser. Nothing you type or generate is ever sent to a server.
