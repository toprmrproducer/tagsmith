About TagSmith

TagSmith is a single-purpose tool for generating gamer tags, usernames, and Discord handles — entirely inside your web browser. Every tag is assembled on your device from hardcoded, curated word lists. Nothing you generate is ever uploaded to a server.

Because there is no server-side processing, TagSmith can stay completely free with no account, no watermark, and no rate limits. The site is supported by on-page advertising.

Pick a vibe — Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Edgy, or Cute — toggle numbers and the classic xX…Xxdecoration on or off, and click Generate as many times as you like. Your last ten tags stay visible so you can compare and copy the one you like best.

Every gamer tag is generated locally in your browser. Nothing you type or generate is ever sent to a server.
